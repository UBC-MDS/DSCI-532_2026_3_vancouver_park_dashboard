# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to welcome participation in our project and ensure an encouraging and friendly experience for every contributor. Discrimination and/or violence are strictly prohibited. We value every effort that contributes to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of accepted behaviors in communication and contribution:

- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using professional and polite language
- Being open-minded for various ideas raised by different people
- Remaining inclusive when facing with different voices (either praise or constructive criticism)

Examples of unaccepted behaviors in our community:

- Offend others by using aggressive languages
- Verbal, mental and/or physical bullying
- Sharing other members' private information to the public, unless explicitly allowed
- Any other unprofessional behaviors

## Enforcement

We as the project administrators reserve the final say of rules and the rights to intervene in conflicts to protect everyone's granted rights. That being said, we may make necessary adjustments to the contents of the project repository, block someone's access or even remove someone from this community if his/her behaviors severely violate this code of conduct.
